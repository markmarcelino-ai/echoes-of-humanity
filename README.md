# Echoes of Humanity

Echoes of Humanity is an AI-assisted storytelling project that explores pivotal moments in human history through short-form videos.

The project combines historical narratives, visual storytelling, and AI-generated imagery to present powerful moments that shaped civilizations.

---

## Concept

Each video focuses on a specific historical event, figure, or cultural moment.

Themes explored include:

• Triumph and tragedy in human history  
• Cultural and civilizational milestones  
• Forgotten stories and lessons from the past  

The goal is to create visually engaging historical storytelling that resonates with modern audiences.
